# Dashboard Calculations, Data Cleaning & Logic

This document describes every calculation, data-cleaning step, and matching rule used by the
Analyst Dashboard (`index.html`) and the iSolved Dashboard (`isolved.html`), both generated by
`build_dashboard.py`. The two pages run identical code; a single `ROLE` constant decides which
CR-sheet column each page groups by.

---

## 1. Data sources

| Report | Detected by columns | Used for |
|---|---|---|
| Connectivity Requests (CR) | `Request ID` | connection cards, production, stage durations |
| Action Items (AI) | `ActionItemID` or `CurrentlyPendingOn` | action items on cards + "other" list |
| OE Requests (OE) | `OERequestID` | in-progress OE section |

Data reaches the page two ways:

- **Embedded at build time** — `build_dashboard.py` reads the Excel exports with pandas and
  embeds the rows as JSON.
- **Uploaded in the browser** — the "Update data" button parses `.xlsx`/`.xls` files with SheetJS.
  Files are auto-recognized by the columns above; unrecognized files are skipped with a message.
  A partial upload keeps the other reports from the previous data.

### Page role

- `index.html`: person = CR `Technical Contact` / OE `TechnicalContact` (the analyst).
- `isolved.html`: person = CR `iSolved Contact` / OE `IsolvedContact`.
- Each card also displays the *other* role's contact.
- All groupings, rankings, and percentages on a page use that page's role column.

## 2. Data cleaning

### 2.1 At build time (`build_dashboard.py`)

- Only whitelisted columns are kept per report (`CR_KEEP`, `AI_KEEP`, `OE_KEEP`).
- All datetime columns are formatted as `YYYY-MM-DD` **as-is** (pandas `strftime`, no timezone math).
- Null cells are dropped from the JSON to shrink the page.
- **Embed mask** — only CR rows the dashboard can use are embedded:
  - status is active (`In Progress`, `Blocked`, `On Hold`, `Not Started`) with a Technical Contact, **or**
  - has a `Ready For Production` or `Production` date (feeds production history), **or**
  - is referenced by an action item (by `ConnectivityRequestID` or by matching client+carrier name).
- OE rows are embedded only when their status is active.

### 2.2 At upload time (browser)

- The workbook is read **without** `cellDates`, so date cells stay raw Excel serial numbers —
  the sheet's literal calendar day with no timezone interpretation (chosen after a bug where
  UTC conversion shifted dates one day back for UTC+ timezones).
- OE rows are filtered to active statuses at read time.

### 2.3 Date parsing (`toISO`)

Every date is normalized to a `YYYY-MM-DD` string:

- **Excel serial number** → pure arithmetic on the Excel epoch (timezone-independent).
- **Date-only string** (`2026-06-08`) → taken as-is.
- **`m/d/yyyy` string** → rearranged as-is.
- **Full timestamp** (e.g. a cached upload whose Dates were JSON-serialized to UTC) → converted
  back to the **local** calendar day.
- **JS `Date` object** (legacy cached data) → local calendar day.

### 2.4 Action-item comment cleaning

- Comments authored by **"System Admin"** are automated due-date reminders: the comment *and its
  date* are ignored entirely (they would otherwise reset pending-day counters).
- Comment line breaks stored as `<br/>` are converted to real line breaks; comments are truncated
  to 500 characters in hover popups.
- An AI with no (human) comment is flagged `*` — its dates count from the AI creation date.

### 2.5 Name normalization (for matching)

- Lowercase; every non-alphanumeric character becomes a space; whitespace collapsed.
- Stop words removed: `inc llc llp ltd co corp corporation company the of`.
- Carrier "base" variant additionally strips any parenthesized part —
  `Third Party (Optum)` matches a CR filed as `Third Party`.

## 3. Action item ↔ CR matching

1. **By ID (authoritative)** — if the AI has `ConnectivityRequestID`, it links only by that.
2. **Name fallback** (older report formats without the ID column), tried in order:
   1. exact normalized `client + carrier` key;
   2. base key (carrier parentheses stripped);
   3. fuzzy match:
      - *clients*: equal, or one is a prefix of the other and both ≥ 20 chars (exports truncate long names);
      - *carriers*: spacing/plural drift (`SunLife`/`Sun Life`), acronyms (`VSP`/`Vision Service Plan`,
        `BCBS`/`Blue Cross Blue Shield`), single-word containment (`Optum` in `HSA w/Optum`),
        or a shared distinctive first word ≥ 5 chars (`Cigna/CBS` ↔ `Cigna Healthcare`).
3. AIs not landing on an active connection card are resolved to *some* CR (live/cancelled/unassigned)
   by the same cascade, preferring a CR that has an assigned contact — this powers links and the
   "other" list.

**Known trade-offs of name matching:** with multiple CRs sharing a client+carrier, an AI can attach
to the wrong one or appear under two cards/analysts; names too different for the fuzzy rules
silently fail to attach.

### "Other open action items" list

Shows open AIs **not already on one of the person's connection cards** where any of:
- the AI's resolved CR belongs to the person;
- the person is the AI's **Requestor**;
- the person is the **responsible party** (`CurrentlyPendingOn`).

Sorted by days pending, descending.

## 4. Time calculations

### 4.1 Calendar days

`daysBetween` = whole days between two `YYYY-MM-DD` dates (UTC midnight to UTC midnight).
"Today" is the data's `generated` date, not the viewer's clock.

### 4.2 Working days — differs per page

Weekend definition:
- **Analyst page**: Friday & Saturday.
- **iSolved page**: Saturday & Sunday.

Full weeks contribute 5 working days each; leftover days are walked one at a time skipping the
weekend days. Reports state the rule in their header (`wd = working days (… excluded)`).

### 4.3 AI pending clock

`effective date = last human comment date, else AI start date`.
Pending days/working-days count from that date to the data date.

### 4.4 Idle time on connections

`last activity = max(any CR milestone/stage date, latest AI effective date)`.

### 4.5 Warning intervals (one rule for all day-based colors)

CR idle chips and AI due-date chips share the same intervals and palette:
`< 4` days ok (green), `4–9` warn (amber), `≥ 10` bad/over (red) —
counting days idle for CRs and days overdue for AI due dates.

## 5. Stage logic

Stage order: Pending Start → Requirements Gathering → Resource Assignment → Dataset Validation →
Mapping → Testing → Ready for Production → Production.
(The retired "Obtain Customer Dataset" stage is mapped onto Dataset Validation.)

- Each stage's **start date** comes from its CR column; `Pending Start` uses `Created Date`.
- **Testing override**: Testing starts at `First Test File` when recorded, else the `Testing`
  column date. A CR at/past Testing with no test file gets a `*` marker on the rail.
- A stage's **duration** = its start → the start of the **next stage that has a date** (missing
  intermediate stages are skipped, so the timeline always tiles with no gaps/overlaps).
- A stage with no later dated stage is "in progress" — it has no completed duration.

---

## 6. Monthly production

- A CR counts toward production in the month of its **Ready For Production date, falling back to
  the Production date** when RFP is empty (`month = date.slice(0,7)`).
- **Team total** for a month = every production CR that month across the whole report — including
  CRs with nobody in the page's role column. The denominator is therefore identical on both pages.
- **Percentage** = person ÷ team total × 100, rounded to 1 decimal; hidden when the team total is 0.
- **Rank** — see §8.

---

## 7. Average stage duration (bottom section)

- **Population**: the person's CRs that reached **Ready For Production or Production**
  (in-flight CRs never count), assigned to the **year of their production date**
  (RFP falling back to Production — the same rule as §6, so the population matches the
  "total production: N CRs in {year}" figure exactly).
- The year dropdown lists the person's production years.
- **All completed stage intervals** of those CRs count toward that year, regardless of which
  year each individual stage finished.
- Stages measured: **Dataset Validation onward** — Pending Start, Requirements Gathering and
  Resource Assignment are excluded by design.
- **Average** = arithmetic mean of interval lengths in days ÷ 7, shown as weeks with 1 decimal.
  Bars are scaled relative to the slowest stage.
- Per-stage CR counts can be below the production total for one reason only: missing stage dates
  in the sheet (no measurable interval).

### Outlier removal

- Assumes a normal distribution per stage: keeps durations within **mean ± z·σ**
  (population standard deviation).
- Confidence selector: 90% (z = 1.645), 95% (z = 1.96), **99% (z = 2.576, default)**, or off.
- Samples with fewer than 3 intervals are never trimmed; σ = 0 skips trimming.
- Removed CRs are listed under the stage (click "N outliers removed"): CR link, client — carrier,
  duration, and the exact date range.

## 8. Ranking

- Used in the monthly production line and the yearly total in the stage-duration header.
- Pool = everyone (in the page's role) with ≥ 1 production CR in the period; people with zero
  are not in the pool.
- **Standard competition ranking ("1224")**: rank = 1 + number of people with a strictly higher
  count; ties share a rank and the next distinct count skips ahead. A rank therefore always means
  "this many people beat them, plus one".
- Clicking the rank opens the full ranking in the report modal (copyable / downloadable), with
  the selected person marked `←`.

---

## 9. Searches

- **Connection search** (toolbar): matches every *active* connection on the page's roster by
  client, carrier, or CR # (with/without `#`), max 15 hits. Picking one switches to the assigned
  person, expands the section if collapsed, scrolls to the card and flash-highlights it.
- **Production search** (production section): same matching over every CR with a production/RFP
  date, all people and months. Picking one switches person **and** month, then jumps to the row.
- Rows with nobody in the page's role column are excluded (no page to jump to).

---

## 10. Caching & refresh

- Uploads are cached in **IndexedDB** (`analystDashDB`), shared by both pages — upload once,
  both views update. (localStorage was abandoned: its ~5 MB quota silently truncated full reports;
  an old localStorage copy is migrated once, then cleared.)
- On load, a cached upload is used only if its date is **strictly newer** than the embedded
  data; otherwise the stale cache is deleted so a fresh build wins.
- The upload date stamp uses the local calendar day.
- If saving fails, the status line shows a visible warning instead of failing silently.

---

## 11. Employee list

A page's dropdown contains the union of: connection assignees, production assignees, OE assignees,
resolved-CR contacts of open AIs, and AI requestors who are also contacts in the page's role.
Sorted with people who have active connections first, then alphabetically.
